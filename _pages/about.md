Welcome! I am **Yihalem Yimolal Tiruneh**, an M.S. researcher at the [Vision & Learning Lab](https://sites.google.com/site/bsrvision00) at the [Ulsan National Institute of Science & Technology (UNIST)](https://www.unist.ac.kr/), advised by [Prof. Seungryul Baek](https://sites.google.com/site/bsrvision00) and co-advised by [Prof. Binod Bhattarai](https://sites.google.com/view/bbinod/home).

My research focuses on **3D human avatar reconstruction**, **3D Gaussian splatting**, and **vision–language reasoning** for hand and human understanding. My recent first-author work (ECCV 2026, under review) introduces a *visibility-aware* 3D Gaussian avatar framework that reconstructs animatable avatars from monocular video across full-body, upper-body, and head-only inputs within a single pipeline — combining occlusion-robust SMPL-X tracking, part-specific residual refinement, and diffusion-based texture completion.

I have additionally contributed to work on hand pose benchmarking ([HandVQA](https://kcsayem.github.io/handvqa/), CVPR 2026), text-conditioned hand–object mesh generation ([THOM](https://uyoung-jeong.github.io/THOM_Project/), CVPR Findings 2026), real-time two-hand manipulation ([QORT-Former](https://kcsayem.github.io/QORT-Former/), AAAI 2025), and Stable-Diffusion-based generative replay for class-incremental detection ([SDDGR](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2402.17323), CVPR 2024 *Highlight*, top 2.8%).

I earned my B.S. in Computer Science and Engineering from UNIST (*Cum Laude*, 2024), supported by the **UNIST Dream Scholarship**, and continue my M.S. on a **Korean Government Graduate School Scholarship** (full tuition + stipend).
